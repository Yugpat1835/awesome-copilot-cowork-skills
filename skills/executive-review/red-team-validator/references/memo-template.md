# Validation Memo Template

Write the memo as Markdown, filename validation-memo-YYYY-MM-DD.md (today's date; append -v2, -v3 on name collision, never overwrite). Follow this structure exactly. Omit nothing; if a section has no findings, write the single line "No findings." under its heading.

---

# DRAFT: Validation memo, [artifact filename]

Generated by red-team-validator on YYYY-MM-DD. Unreviewed until a human signs off.

## Overall verdict

[PASS | PASS WITH CORRECTIONS | FAIL]

- **PASS:** zero UNSUPPORTED, zero CONTRADICTED, and filler flags do not affect any decision-relevant statement. UNVERIFIABLE claims may exist if they are clearly framed as external or forward-looking.
- **PASS WITH CORRECTIONS:** UNSUPPORTED or filler findings exist but no CONTRADICTED verdicts; the document is sendable once the corrections section is worked through. Also the ceiling whenever any section was sampled rather than fully checked.
- **FAIL:** one or more CONTRADICTED verdicts, or UNSUPPORTED verdicts on the claims the document's main argument rests on.

State the counts on one line, for example: 23 claims checked: 17 supported, 4 unsupported, 1 contradicted, 1 unverifiable. 6 filler flags. 2 internal contradictions.

## Scope

- Artifact under review: [full path]
- In-scope sources: [bulleted list of full paths; note "org-profile.md: not found" if absent; name any source the user cited that could not be opened]
- Web check: [not authorised | authorised by user; EXTERNAL results marked in the table]
- Coverage: [fully checked | sections sampled: list them]

## Verdict table

| ID | Claim (verbatim) | Location | Verdict | Where checked / evidence | Suggested correction |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| C01 | ... | ... | SUPPORTED | [source, exact location; show recomputation for derived figures] | none |
| C02 | ... | ... | UNSUPPORTED | [sources and sections searched] | remove or source this claim |

One row per claim from the inventory, in document order. Verdicts in capitals. Never leave the evidence column blank.

## Filler flags

Numbered list. Each entry: the sentence verbatim, its location, the test it fails (transplant, negation, or named pattern), and the suggested correction (a specific replacement fact with citation, or "delete; adds nothing").

## Internal contradictions

Numbered list. Each entry quotes both conflicting statements verbatim with both locations, and states which side (if either) the in-scope data supports.

## Corrections, in priority order

1. CONTRADICTED claims first: what to change and to what, citing the supporting source.
2. UNSUPPORTED claims on load-bearing points: source them or cut them.
3. Filler in decision-relevant sections: the replacement facts.
4. Everything else.

Where the right figure exists in an in-scope source, state it with its citation. Where it does not, the correction is removal or sourcing, never a guessed number.

---

End of template. Do not add praise sections, summaries of strengths, or softening preambles. The conversation report after saving is one paragraph: overall verdict, the counts line, and the full saved path.
